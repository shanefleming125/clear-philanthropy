MetricCurrent YearPrior YearTwo Years Ago
Total Revenue ?
Total Revenue
All income received during the fiscal year — grants, donations, program fees, government contracts, investment income, and other sources combined.
Where to find it
BEST Audited financials — Statement of Activities, Total Revenue line
990 Form 990, Part VIII, Line 12 (Total Revenue)
OTHER Income statement or internal P&L — top-line total
Total Expenses ?
Total Expenses
All money spent during the fiscal year across program, management, and fundraising activities.
Where to find it
BEST Audited financials — Statement of Activities, Total Expenses line
990 Form 990, Part IX, Line 25 (Total Functional Expenses)
OTHER Income statement or internal P&L — total expenses line
Total Assets ?
Total Assets
Everything the organization owns or controls — cash, receivables, property, investments, and equipment at year-end.
Where to find it
BEST Audited financials — Balance Sheet (Statement of Financial Position), Total Assets
990 Form 990, Part X, Line 16 (Total Assets)
OTHER Internal balance sheet — total assets line
Total Liabilities ?
Total Liabilities
Everything the organization owes — accounts payable, deferred revenue, loans, and other obligations at year-end.
Where to find it
BEST Audited financials — Balance Sheet, Total Liabilities
990 Form 990, Part X, Line 26 (Total Liabilities)
OTHER Internal balance sheet — total liabilities line
Cash & Cash Equivalents ?
Cash & Cash Equivalents
Immediately accessible funds — checking, savings, money market accounts, and short-term instruments convertible to cash within 90 days.
Where to find it
BEST Audited financials — Balance Sheet, Cash and Cash Equivalents (top of asset section)
990 Form 990, Part X, Line 1 (Cash — non-interest bearing) + Line 2 (Savings and temporary cash investments)
OTHER Internal balance sheet or bank statements at year-end
Current Assets ?
Current Assets
Assets expected to be converted to cash within 12 months — includes cash, receivables, prepaid expenses, and short-term investments.
Where to find it
BEST Audited financials — Balance Sheet, "Current Assets" subtotal (explicitly labeled)
990 Form 990 does not label "current assets" — add Part X Lines 1–4 as an estimate (cash + receivables)
OTHER Internal balance sheet — current assets subtotal, or ask the org directly
Current Liabilities ?
Current Liabilities
Obligations due within 12 months — accounts payable, accrued expenses, short-term debt, and deferred revenue expected to be earned this year.
Where to find it
BEST Audited financials — Balance Sheet, "Current Liabilities" subtotal (explicitly labeled)
990 Form 990 does not label "current liabilities" — use Part X Lines 17–18 as an estimate (accounts payable + deferred revenue)
OTHER Internal balance sheet — current liabilities subtotal, or ask the org directly
Program Service Expenses ?
Program Service Expenses
Money spent directly delivering the mission — staff, supplies, and overhead tied to programs and services. Excludes management and fundraising costs.
Where to find it
BEST Audited financials — Statement of Functional Expenses, Program Services column total
990 Form 990, Part IX, Line 25, Column B (Program Service Expenses)
OTHER Internal expense report — program or direct service costs
Management & General Expenses ?
Management & General Expenses
Overhead costs not tied to programs or fundraising — executive leadership, finance, HR, legal, IT, and general administration.
Where to find it
BEST Audited financials — Statement of Functional Expenses, Management & General column total
990 Form 990, Part IX, Line 25, Column C (Management and General)
OTHER Internal expense report — admin or overhead cost category
Fundraising Expenses ?
Fundraising Expenses
Costs specifically incurred to solicit donations and grants — events, direct mail, donor stewardship, and development staff time allocated to fundraising.
Where to find it
BEST Audited financials — Statement of Functional Expenses, Fundraising column total
990 Form 990, Part IX, Line 25, Column D (Fundraising)
OTHER Internal expense report — development or fundraising cost category
Total Contributions (Donations) ?
Total Contributions
All philanthropic support received — individual donations, foundation grants, corporate giving, and government grants. Does not include earned revenue like program fees.
Where to find it
BEST Audited financials — Statement of Activities, Contributions and Grants line (or sum of all contribution types)
990 Form 990, Part VIII, Line 1h (Total Contributions, Gifts, Grants)
OTHER Internal revenue report — donations and grants received
Largest Single Revenue Source ?
Largest Single Revenue Source
The single largest revenue line item — one funder, one contract, or one revenue stream. Used to measure concentration risk. If one source disappears, how exposed is the org?
Where to find it
BEST Audited financials — Schedule of Grants and Contributions, or notes to financial statements listing major funders
990 Form 990, Part VIII — scan Lines 1a–1f and other revenue lines to identify the largest single item. Schedule B lists major donors (>$5K) but is not public.
OTHER Ask the org directly: "What is your largest single source of revenue and how much did it represent last year?"
