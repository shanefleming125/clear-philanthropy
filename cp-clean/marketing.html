Who We Are

Built for Funders With Lean Teams.

Most foundations doing meaningful work don't have a large program staff. They have one or two people — sometimes none — carrying the weight of every grant decision, every grantee relationship, and every reporting cycle.

Clear Philanthropy exists for those funders. We step in as external program support — not a vendor you manage, but a partner who understands your vision, represents your values, and helps you give with the same rigor and intentionality as foundations ten times your size.

We're not a platform. We're not a report mill. We're the program capacity your foundation needs, without the overhead of a full-time hire.

"We step in as an extension of your team — representing your vision, your values, and your commitment to giving well."
— Clear Philanthropy
