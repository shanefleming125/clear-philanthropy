The Reality

Most Foundations Are Making Serious Decisions With Very Little Support.

A foundation making half a million dollars in grants a year might have one staff person — or none. They care deeply about where their money goes. But between the relationships, the reporting, the board, and the day-to-day, there's rarely enough bandwidth to give every decision the rigor it deserves.

That's not a failure of intent. It's a resource problem. And it's exactly the kind of problem Clear Philanthropy was built to solve.

We work alongside funders as external program support — experienced, independent, and fully oriented around your vision and values. Not a vendor you manage. A partner who shows up.

"We're not a platform. We're not a report mill. We're the program capacity your foundation needs — built around your work."
What We Do

Support at Every Stage of the Giving Cycle.

Whether you need help at one moment or across the full arc of a grant relationship, CP brings the same rigor and care to every stage.

A structured financial and organizational health review — scored, interpreted, and delivered as a clear, shareable summary that gives you confidence in your decision.

Who We Are

Practitioner Experience, In Your Corner.

Clear Philanthropy was built by someone who has done this work — managing grant portfolios, conducting site visits, building relationships with nonprofit leaders, and preparing board materials. Not an algorithm. Not a platform that generates reports. A practitioner who brings real experience to every engagement.

That matters because grantmaking at its best isn't purely analytical. It requires judgment about organizations, people, and context that doesn't come from a spreadsheet. CP brings both — the rigor of a documented methodology and the instincts that come from actually being in the room.

We represent your vision and values. Not our own theory of change. Not a one-size-fits-all framework. Yours.

Independent
CP works on behalf of the funder, not the grantee. Our assessments reflect what the data shows — not what anyone hopes it will say.
Practitioner-led
Every engagement is informed by real program officer experience — the kind of judgment that comes from actually sitting across the table from nonprofit leaders.
Values-driven
CP doesn't impose a theory of change. We surface what the data shows and support decisions that reflect what you're trying to accomplish as a funder.
Relational
The best grantmaking is built on trust — with grantees, with communities, with boards. CP brings a relational approach to every stage of the work.
