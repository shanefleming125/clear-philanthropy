Why it matters

Great grantmaking starts with understanding who you're funding.

990s don't tell the full story
Tax filings are 12–18 months old by the time you read them. They capture what happened — not what's happening now. The organizations you're considering today have evolved since their last filing.
Declined without explanation
Most foundations decline organizations without feedback — not because they don't care, but because translating financial data into constructive language is genuinely hard. Organizations are left wondering why, unable to improve.
Due diligence without infrastructure
Many foundation teams are doing serious grantmaking without the systems to support it. Every reviewer does it differently. Assessment quality varies. Decisions are hard to defend — to your board, and to yourself.
We genuinely wanted to help organizations improve. But we didn't know how to translate what we were seeing in the numbers into something we could share with them.
— Foundation Program Officer
What Clear Philanthropy does

Structured assessment. Plain language. Actionable findings.

We built the tool we always wished existed — one that takes the complexity out of due diligence and puts clarity back into the conversation between funders and the organizations they support.

Financial health assessment, built for grantmakers
Eight key financial ratios, automatically calculated from current org-submitted data. A weighted health score from 0–100. Three years of trend analysis. Everything you need to assess financial readiness — without needing a finance background.
Current data, not stale filings
Assessment narratives that give you the language to act
Every assessment generates a plain-language funding memo — decline with feedback, conditional approval, or invite to apply. Finally, the words to communicate what you're seeing, in a way that's honest, constructive, and genuinely useful to the organization.
Closes the feedback gap
Better conversations with the organizations you fund
When your team understands what the numbers mean, everything changes. You ask better questions. You can explain a decline. You build more honest, trust-based relationships with applicants — and the organizations you support feel that difference.
Stronger relationships
How it works

From intake to decision — in one place.

A complete workflow for your team, from first contact to final recommendation.

01
Org submits their data
You share a branded intake link. The organization fills out their financial information directly — current, accurate, from the source.
→
02
Platform runs the assessment
Eight ratios calculated automatically. Health score generated. Trend analysis across three years. Financial health indicators flagged.
→
03
You add your judgment
Review the findings, add your notes and observations, select a preliminary recommendation. The platform supports your thinking — it doesn't replace it.
→
04
Share with confidence
Export a board-ready PDF report. Share the assessment narrative with the organization. Make your decision knowing you've done the work thoroughly.
