What We Do

Independent Assessment. Before the Process Begins.

Current, Not Stale
Public filings are a starting point. CP collects current financial data directly from the organization — so your assessment reflects where they are today, not 18 months ago.
Interpretation, Not Just Data

How It Works

Simple for Funders. Respectful for Organizations.

A structured process that works for everyone — and keeps the focus on what matters.

1
Funder Identifies an Organization
A foundation, DAF advisor, or corporate giving team flags an org they want to know more about.
2
CP Sends an Intake Link
The organization receives a simple, guided intake request — current financials, governance basics, and key organizational information.
3
CP Runs the Assessment
Financial ratios, three-year trends, governance indicators, and risk factors — reviewed, scored, and interpreted.
4
Funder Receives a Clear Summary
A concise assessment with scoring, narrative analysis, key strengths and risks, and recommended next steps.
5
Funder Decides What Comes Next
Invite a proposal, continue the conversation, request more information, or move forward — with a clear picture of the organization behind the ask.
What You Receive

Financial Health
