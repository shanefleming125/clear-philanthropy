The Reality

Most Foundations Are Making Serious Decisions With Very Little Support.

A foundation making half a million dollars in grants a year might have one staff person — or none. They care deeply about where their money goes. But between the relationships, the reporting, the board, and the day-to-day, there's rarely enough bandwidth to give every decision the rigor it deserves.

That's not a failure of intent. It's a resource problem. And it's exactly the kind of problem Clear Philanthropy was built to solve.

We work alongside funders as external program support — experienced, independent, and fully oriented around your vision and values. Not a vendor you manage. A partner who shows up.

"We're not a platform. We're not a report mill. We're the program capacity your foundation needs — built around your work."

About This Assessment

This report was prepared by Clear Philanthropy, an external program support practice that helps foundations and philanthropic funders assess nonprofit financial health and make more informed grant decisions.

The Health Score is a weighted composite of 8 financial ratios derived from established nonprofit sector benchmarks and practitioner standards — including liquidity, operating performance, program efficiency, leverage, revenue diversification, and net asset position.

This assessment reflects the financial data available at the time of review. It is designed to surface material financial patterns and prompt informed inquiry — not to render a definitive judgment on organizational merit or mission impact.

Who We Are

We've Worked on Every Side of Philanthropy.

Clear Philanthropy is a team of professionals who have worked across the full spectrum of the philanthropic sector — with extensive grantmaking experience and equally deep roots in direct service delivery.

That combination matters. Our direct service background shapes how we show up with the organizations we work with. We understand the operational realities, the resource constraints, and the day-to-day pressures that define nonprofit life from the inside. When we sit down with a grantee, we meet them where they are. Because we've been there.

We represent your vision and values — not our own. We bring the judgment. You make the call.

Independent
CP works on behalf of the funder, not the grantee. Our assessments reflect what the data shows — not what anyone hopes it will say.
Practitioner-led
Every engagement is informed by real program officer and direct service experience — the kind of judgment that comes from actually being in the room.
Values-driven
CP doesn't impose a theory of change. We surface what the data shows and support decisions that reflect what you're trying to accomplish as a funder.
Relational
The best grantmaking is built on trust — with grantees, with communities, with boards. CP brings a relational approach to every stage of the work.
