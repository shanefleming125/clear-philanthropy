Where we fit

Grant management systems handle the process. Clear Philanthropy handles what comes before it.

Organizational readiness is asked late
The full proposal process is resource-intensive for everyone involved. A structured pre-grant diagnostic lets funders understand an organization's readiness earlier — before either side has invested heavily in the relationship.
Available data doesn't tell the full story
Public filings are a starting point — not a complete picture. An independent diagnostic built on current, org-submitted data gives funders a more accurate view of where an organization stands today, and what questions are worth exploring further.
Feedback is valuable — and hard to give
Translating financial data into constructive, actionable language is genuinely difficult. A structured assessment narrative gives funders the framework to communicate findings clearly — whether the next step is an invitation, a conversation, or a referral to capacity support.

What Clear Philanthropy provides

An independent diagnostic. Before the process begins.

Like a doctor running tests before recommending treatment — the diagnostic doesn't judge the patient. It informs the conversation. The funder decides what to do with it.

Financial health assessment
Eight key financial ratios calculated from current, org-submitted data. A weighted health score from 0–100. Three years of trend analysis. Not a judgment — a picture of where the organization stands financially, right now.
Current data, not stale filings
Plain-language assessment narrative
Every diagnostic produces a structured narrative — what the data shows, what questions it raises, and what a funder might want to explore further. Written to be shared. Finally, the language to have an honest, constructive conversation with an applicant.
Closes the feedback gap
A complete pre-grant workflow
Organizations submit data through a branded intake form. Your team reviews the diagnostic, adds observations, and exports a board-ready report. One place for everything that happens before the proposal process begins.
End-to-end pre-grant process
How it works

Four steps. Before the proposal ever arrives.

A structured process that respects everyone's time — yours, and the organization's.

01
Share the intake link
Send a branded link to the organization. They submit their current financial data directly — no 990 hunting, no spreadsheet requests.
→
02
Diagnostic runs automatically
Eight financial ratios calculated. Health score generated. Trends analyzed across three years. Indicators flagged. All from the data they submitted.
→
03
Reviewer adds context
Your team reviews the findings, adds observations and questions, and generates an assessment narrative. The platform informs your judgment — it doesn't replace it.
→
04
Decide with confidence
Invite to apply, ask for more information, or decline with feedback that actually helps. Every decision backed by a clear, documented diagnostic.

Who benefits

A clearer process benefits everyone in it.

Pre-grant due diligence serves funders and organizations equally. It's also a natural fit for the growing field of trust-based philanthropy — where the question isn't "do we trust this org?" but "what does the data tell us, and how do we build from here?"

For funders
  • Understand organizational readiness earlier in the process
  • Ground funding decisions in current, structured data
  • Communicate findings clearly — invitation, conditions, or referral to capacity support
  • Build a consistent, documented due diligence process your board can follow
  • Support trust-based models with objective, independent diagnostics
For organizations
  • Receive a clear picture of how their financial health reads to funders
  • Get structured, actionable feedback — not just a decision
  • Understand what to strengthen before the next funding cycle
  • Enter funding conversations with greater self-awareness and confidence
  • Build trust with funders through transparency and shared language
